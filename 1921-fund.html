The 1921 Fund

Purpose

Serves as the financial engine for fostering community growth and resilience.

Impact Goals

Develop a $100M fund. Disburse millions annually to transformative community projects.

What We Offer

Community Development Grants: Funding for local infrastructure, health, and social well-being projects.
Educational Scholarships: For future leaders in entrepreneurship, technology, and financial literacy.
Social Impact Investments: Financial support for ventures addressing challenges like housing, workforce development, and sustainability.
