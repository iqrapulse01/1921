About The 1921 Project

Introduction

The 1921 Project is an ambitious initiative of the Urban Entrepreneurs Institute (UEI) and the brainchild of the business development think tank Business Minds Working (BMW).

Purpose

Inspired by the resilience of Black Wall Street, it is dedicated to building a billion-dollar ecosystem that empowers entrepreneurs and revitalizes communities.

Impact Goals

Empower 1,000+ entrepreneurs by 2030.

Generate $1B+ in economic impact through business development and community investment.

Create sustainable ecosystems that inspire innovation and resilience.
