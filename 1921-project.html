About The 1921 Project

Our Vision

Economic Empowerment: Provide resources and funding to entrepreneurs and communities.
Sustainable Growth: Foster ventures that drive long-term social and economic impact.
Honoring Resilience: Celebrating the legacy of Tulsa Oklahoma's Black Wall Street.

What We Offer

Startup Incubation: Support for ideation, prototyping, and market validation.
Business Accelerator: Resources focused on scaling high-potential startups.
Workshops and Training: Courses on business planning, digital marketing, and leadership.
Community Engagement: Collaboration with peers, mentors, and investors.

Impact Goals

Launch and support 500+ businesses by 2030.

Build a pipeline of sustainable enterprises that create jobs, drive economic growth, and address community needs.
