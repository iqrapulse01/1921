1921 BC (Business Cultivator)

Purpose

A business incubator and accelerator providing structured programs, mentorship, and resources to help entrepreneurs build thriving businesses.

Impact Goals

Support 500+ businesses by 2030.

What We Offer

Startup Incubation: Support for ideation, prototyping, and market validation.
Business Accelerator: Resources for scaling startups, funding opportunities, and growth strategies.
Workshops and Training: Courses on business planning, financial literacy, digital marketing, and leadership.
Community Engagement: Collaboration opportunities with mentors and investors.
